Pressure Tank Blowdown Equations (Choked + Subsonic)

gamma_air = 1.4
critical_ratio = (2/(gamma + 1))^(gamma/(gamma - 1))
critical_ratio_air = 0.528
choked_if = (P_down/P_tank) <= critical_ratio
choked_if_equiv = (P_tank/P_down) >= ((gamma + 1)/2)^(gamma/(gamma - 1))

mdot_choked = C_d*A_throat*P_tank*sqrt(gamma/(R*T_tank))*(2/(gamma + 1))^((gamma + 1)/(2*(gamma - 1)))

mdot_subsonic = C_d*A_throat*P_tank*sqrt((2*gamma/(R*T_tank*(gamma - 1)))*((P_down/P_tank)^(2/gamma) - (P_down/P_tank)^((gamma + 1)/gamma)))

mass_balance = dm_tank/dt = -mdot
ideal_gas_tank = P_tank = m_tank*R*T_tank/V_tank

adiabatic_relation = P_tank*V_tank^gamma = const
adiabatic_TP = T_tank/T0 = (P_tank/P0)^((gamma - 1)/gamma)
adiabatic_Pm = P_tank/P0 = (m_tank/m0)^gamma

isothermal_relation = T_tank = const
isothermal_Pm = P_tank/P0 = m_tank/m0

time_to_target_pressure = t = integral(from m0 to mf) [dm/(-mdot(m))]

mass_step = m_{k+1} = m_k - mdot_k*dt
pressure_step = P_{k+1} = m_{k+1}*R*T_{k+1}/V_tank
temperature_step_adiabatic = T_{k+1} = T_k*(m_{k+1}/m_k)^(gamma - 1)


Symbol Table

gamma = ratio of specific heats (cp/cv)
gamma_air = ratio of specific heats for air (1.4)
critical_ratio = critical pressure ratio for choking
critical_ratio_air = numeric critical ratio for air (~0.528)
P_tank = upstream tank absolute pressure
P_down = downstream absolute pressure (usually atmospheric)
T_tank = tank absolute temperature
T0 = initial tank temperature
P0 = initial tank pressure
m_tank = gas mass in tank
m0 = initial gas mass in tank
mf = final gas mass at target pressure
V_tank = tank internal volume
C_d = discharge coefficient of orifice/nozzle
A_throat = effective throat/orifice flow area
R = specific gas constant for the gas
mdot = mass flow rate out of tank
mdot_choked = choked-flow mass flow rate
mdot_subsonic = subsonic-flow mass flow rate
dt = simulation time step
m_k = tank mass at step k
m_{k+1} = tank mass at next step
P_{k+1} = tank pressure at next step
T_{k+1} = tank temperature at next step
const = constant value during a given thermodynamic path
